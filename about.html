Vision

At Motion Magic Studios, our vision is to be the unrivaled architects of visual storytelling, transcending the ordinary and sculpting moments that endure through time. Since our inception in 2010 by Cache Crusaders, we have evolved into one of Cape Town's foremost photography businesses. Our expansive team, comprising highly specialized videographers, photographers, and editors, collaborates seamlessly to weave narratives that resonate with authenticity.

Mission

Our mission is to provide an enjoyable experience in front of the camera as well as timeless photographs that you will treasure for a lifetime.
