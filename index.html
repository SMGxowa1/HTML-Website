Welcome to Motion Magic Studios


Motion Magic Studios was founded by Cache Crusaders in 2010. Over the years it has grown into one of the leading photography businesses in Cape Town. The company includes a varying team of videographers, photographer and editors who are highly specialized. We also have a partner network of photographers in most major cities of South Africa. At our studio, we believe in capturing every moment with passion and creativity. From weddings to family portraits, corporate events to commercial projects, we tailor our approach to suit your unique vision.

With state-of-the art equipment and a dedication to excellence, we guarantee stunning results that you’ll cherish for a lifetime.  With 13 years of experience in the photography industry, no job is too big or too small for us. We deliver quality and we’re fast! We work as a team in high-pressure situations and always go the extra mile. Ready to create picture-perfect moments for your next event? Contact us to book a shoot or request a quote. Your vision is our priority, and we're here to turn it into reality.
