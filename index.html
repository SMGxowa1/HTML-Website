Welcome to Motion Magic Studios


Motion Magic Studios was founded by Cache Crusaders in 2010. Over the years it has grown into one of the leading photography businesses in Cape Town. The company includes a varying team of videographers, photographer and editors who are highly specialized. We also have a partner network of photographers in most major cities of South Africa.

About us

We love photography because there is potential to grow every day you go to work.
We love the opportunity to end a day feeling more skilled and better prepared than when I woke up in the morning.

Mission

Our mission is to provide an enjoyable experience in front of the camera as well as timeless photographs that you will treasure for a lifetime.

Goal

Our aim is to capture moments on film and use images to tell stories, creating visual narratives that evoke emotions and convey messages.
